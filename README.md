# 🎮 PS RAFi 007

A collection of interactive web games built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Play multiple games directly in your browser with no downloads required!

## 🎯 Games Included

### 1. **Tic Tac Toe** ⭕
- Classic strategy game
- Play against AI computer
- Try to get three in a row

### 2. **Snake Game** 🐍
- Navigate the snake through the grid
- Eat food to grow longer
- Avoid hitting walls and yourself
- Track your high score

### 3. **Memory Game** 🧠
- Match pairs of cards
- Test your memory skills
- Count your moves

### 4. **Flappy Bird** 🐦
- Tap or click to make the bird flap
- Navigate through pipes
- Don't hit the obstacles
- Beat your high score

### 5. **Pong Game** 🏓
- Classic paddle game
- Play against the computer AI
- Score points by avoiding the ball
- Move your paddle up and down

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click on any game card to start playing
3. Follow the on-screen controls and instructions

## 📱 Features

- ✅ Fully responsive design
- ✅ Works on desktop and mobile
- ✅ No external dependencies
- ✅ Local high score tracking (using localStorage)
- ✅ Beautiful gradient UI
- ✅ Smooth animations
- ✅ Easy navigation

## 🎮 Controls

### Tic Tac Toe
- Click on empty cells to place your mark

### Snake
- Arrow Keys or WASD to move

### Memory Game
- Click cards to find matching pairs

### Flappy Bird
- Click or Press Space to flap

### Pong
- Move mouse up/down to control paddle
